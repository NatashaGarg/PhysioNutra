Understanding Physiotherapy at PhysioNutra Clinic

Physiotherapy, also known as physical therapy, is a branch of healthcare that focuses on restoring and maintaining physical functionality, mobility, and overall well-being. It involves a range of evidence-based techniques and therapeutic exercises designed to alleviate pain, improve flexibility, and enhance strength. From joint mobilization and therapeutic exercises to manual therapy, sports physiotherapy and patient education, our expert physiotherapists at PhysioNutra Clinic employ a diverse set of advanced tools and techniques to cater to the unique needs of each patient in Zirakpur and surrounding areas.
