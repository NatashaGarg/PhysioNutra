How It Works

Your Treatment Journey at PhysioNutra Zirakpur

A structured, evidence-based process from your first assessment through to full recovery and prevention

1

Comprehensive Initial Assessment (Session 1)

Dr. Tarun Garg conducts a thorough evaluation — reviewing your medical history, investigations, and surgical reports; assessing range of motion, strength, neurological status, and functional capacity. Clear, measurable recovery goals and a personalised treatment timeline are established at your very first visit.

2

Active Treatment Phase

Targeted, hands-on physiotherapy using the right combination for your diagnosis and stage of recovery — manual therapy, electrotherapy, dry needling, Class IV Laser, progressive exercise rehabilitation, and sport-specific training as appropriate.

3

Objective Progress Monitoring

Your progress is measured at every session against objective benchmarks — strength symmetry indices, pain scores, range of motion, and functional capacity. Your programme is reviewed and updated accordingly at each visit, not after a fixed number of sessions.

4

Structured Home Exercise Programme

Between clinic sessions, a tailored home exercise programme is prescribed and progressed at each visit. Adherence to this programme is fundamental to achieving lasting results and is a core component of your rehabilitation plan.

5

Discharge & Long-Term Prevention Plan

When your goals are achieved, you receive a personalised maintenance and injury prevention plan. For athletes, this includes objective return-to-sport clearance testing. Our door remains open for any future concerns without the need to restart from scratch.
