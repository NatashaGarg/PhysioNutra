Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about physiotherapy in Zirakpur

What conditions can physiotherapy treat in Zirakpur?

Physiotherapy effectively treats back pain, neck pain, sports injuries, arthritis, post-surgical recovery, frozen shoulder, sciatica, knee pain, and many other musculoskeletal conditions. Our Zirakpur clinic specializes in personalized treatment plans for each condition.

How many physiotherapy sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies based on your condition, severity, and response to treatment. Acute injuries may require 6-8 sessions, while chronic conditions might need 12-15 sessions. After your initial assessment, we'll provide a customized treatment plan with expected duration.

Do I need a doctor's referral for physiotherapy?

No, you don't need a referral to visit our Zirakpur clinic. You can book directly for an assessment. However, if you have a referral from your doctor, it helps us understand your medical history better.

What should I wear to my physiotherapy appointment?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement. For lower body treatments, shorts or track pants work well. For upper body or back treatments, a loose t-shirt is ideal.
