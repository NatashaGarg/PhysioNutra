We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement. For lower back or leg treatments, wear shorts or loose pants. For shoulder or neck treatments, wear a tank top or t-shirt. You may be asked to change into a gown for certain treatments.
5. Is physiotherapy painful?
Physiotherapy should not be excessively painful. You may experience some mild discomfort during certain treatments or exercises, especially in the initial sessions. This is normal and indicates that the treatment is working. Our physiotherapists always work within your comfort level and adjust the treatment intensity accordingly.

8. What is the difference between physiotherapy and massage?
While massage is one technique used in physiotherapy, physiotherapy is a complete medical treatment approach that includes assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of musculoskeletal and neurological conditions.
