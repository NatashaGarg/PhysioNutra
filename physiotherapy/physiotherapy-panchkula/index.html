Your Physiotherapy Journey at PhysioNutra Panchkula

Step 1: Initial Assessment (30-45 minutes)

Your first visit includes a comprehensive evaluation:

  • Review of medical history and current symptoms
  • Physical examination and movement analysis
  • Diagnostic tests to identify root causes
  • Discussion of lifestyle and activity levels
  • Addressing all your questions and concerns

Step 2: Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on assessment, we create a customized plan including:

  • Specific treatment techniques and modalities
  • Recommended frequency and duration
  • Home exercise program
  • Nutritional recommendations
  • Expected recovery timeline
  • Transparent cost estimate

Step 3: Active Treatment

Your treatment sessions may include:

  • Manual Therapy: Joint mobilization, soft tissue massage, myofascial release
  • Therapeutic Exercises: Strengthening, stretching, functional exercises
  • Electrotherapy: TENS, ultrasound, interferential therapy
  • Heat/Cold Therapy: To reduce inflammation and promote healing
  • Dry Needling: Trigger point release and muscle relaxation
  • Taping & Bracing: Support and pain management

Step 4: Progress Monitoring

We regularly assess progress and adjust treatment for optimal results, providing guidance on:

  • Activity modification during recovery
  • Ergonomic corrections for work and home
  • Injury prevention strategies
  • Long-term lifestyle modifications

How many sessions will I need?
Session numbers vary by condition. Acute conditions may need 5-8 sessions, while chronic conditions might require 10-15 sessions. After initial assessment, we'll provide an estimated treatment duration.
What should I wear to my appointment?
Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement. For lower body treatments, wear shorts or loose pants. For upper body treatments, wear a tank top or t-shirt.
Is physiotherapy painful?
Physiotherapy should not be excessively painful. You may experience mild discomfort during certain treatments, which is normal. Our physiotherapists always work within your comfort level.
Can physiotherapy help with chronic pain?
Yes! Physiotherapy is highly effective for managing chronic pain conditions like chronic back pain, arthritis, and fibromyalgia. Our comprehensive approach addresses root causes for long-term relief.
Do you treat sports injuries?
Absolutely! Sports physiotherapy is our specialty. We treat all types of sports injuries including sprains, ACL tears, meniscus injuries, rotator cuff injuries, and more.
