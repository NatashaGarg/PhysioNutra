Physiotherapy in Chandigarh — FAQs

Common questions about our physiotherapy services in Chandigarh

Yes. PhysioNutra Clinic provides comprehensive home physiotherapy across all sectors of Chandigarh. Our physiotherapists arrive fully equipped with portable professional equipment — TENS, IFT, ultrasound, exercise tools, and manual therapy instruments — and deliver the same quality care as our Zirakpur clinic. Call +91 94177 91833 to book.
Home sessions range from ₹1,200–₹1,800 per visit depending on condition complexity. Package rates: 10 sessions for ₹11,000 and 20 sessions for ₹20,000. All equipment and treatment are included with no hidden charges. A free first consultation is available for new patients. Call +91 94177 91833 for personalised pricing.
Session count depends on your condition and severity. During the initial assessment, we provide a clear, personalised treatment plan with an estimated timeline. Most acute conditions show significant improvement within 4–8 sessions. Chronic or post-surgical conditions typically require 12–20+ sessions for full recovery. We never add sessions unnecessarily — your progress guides the plan.
A prescription is helpful but not mandatory. Our qualified physiotherapists can independently assess your condition and begin treatment without a referral. For post-surgical cases, neurological conditions, or insurance reimbursement claims, a doctor’s referral or discharge summary is recommended and we can help co-ordinate with your treating physician.
Same-day appointments are available based on therapist availability. For urgent cases, visits can often be arranged within 2–4 hours of calling. Regular appointments can be scheduled at early morning, evening, or weekend slots. Call +91 94177 91833 to check current availability — we will always try to accommodate you as quickly as possible.
Modern physiotherapy is designed to be comfortable. You may feel mild discomfort during certain manual therapy techniques or therapeutic exercises, but we always work within your pain tolerance and adjust accordingly. Pain during treatment is never necessary — open communication with your therapist ensures every session is appropriate and productive.
