Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the nearest PhysioNutra clinic to me?
Use our location finder above or call +91-9417791833 and share your location. We'll direct you to the nearest clinic. We have locations in Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, Zirakpur, Kharar, and Dera Bassi.
Which PhysioNutra clinic should I visit?
Choose the clinic closest to your home or workplace. All our clinics offer the same quality of care with experienced physiotherapists and modern equipment.
Do all locations offer the same services?
Yes, all PhysioNutra clinics offer comprehensive physiotherapy services including sports injury treatment, back pain management, post-surgical rehabilitation, and specialized treatments.
Can I get home physiotherapy instead of visiting a clinic?
Absolutely! We offer home physiotherapy services across all areas in Tricity. Our physiotherapists visit your home with all necessary equipment. Call +91-9417791833 to book a home visit.
Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?
While we accept walk-ins based on availability, we recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting time. Call or WhatsApp +91-9417791833 to schedule your visit.
Is parking available at all locations?
Yes, all our clinic locations have parking facilities available for patients.
What are the clinic timings?
All PhysioNutra clinics are open Monday to Saturday from 9 AM to 7 PM, and Sunday from 10 AM to 2 PM.
How much does a physiotherapy session cost?
Physiotherapy sessions range from ₹500 to ₹1500 depending on the condition and treatment. We offer package deals for multiple sessions at discounted rates.
