Understanding Your Chronic Lower Back Pain and Symptoms

Chronic pain is typically defined as pain lasting longer than 12 weeks. Unlike acute pain from an injury, chronic back pain often stems from underlying issues like poor posture, muscular imbalances, disc degeneration, or conditions like Sciatica and Spondylosis. Our initial assessment focuses on diagnosing the specific source of your discomfort.

Common Causes of Chronic Back Pain We Treat:

  • Lumbar Spondylosis/Osteoarthritis: Degeneration of the spinal discs and joints.
  • Sciatica: Pain radiating down the leg due to nerve compression.
  • Postural Syndrome: Muscle strain from prolonged sitting or standing habits.
  • Disc Protrusion/Herniation: Damage to the intervertebral discs.

Personalized Physiotherapy for Lasting Back Pain Relief

Our treatment for chronic back pain management is holistic and tailored to your specific needs. It aims to reduce inflammation, restore mobility, and build the core strength necessary to support the spine, preventing future pain episodes.

Core Treatment Techniques at PhysioNutra Clinic:

  1. Spine Mobilization & Manipulation: Gentle, precise hands-on techniques to restore normal joint movement and reduce stiffness.
  2. Therapeutic & Core Strengthening Exercises: Focused programs to build stability in the back, abdomen, and pelvis (the "Powerhouse").
  3. Advanced Modalities: Application of advanced equipment like Ultrasound or TENS for immediate pain and inflammation reduction.
  4. Postural and Ergonomic Correction: Education to modify daily habits and prevent recurrence of pain.
