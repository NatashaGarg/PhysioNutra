Understanding Your Cervical Pain (Neck Pain) and Symptoms

Â  Â  Â  Â 

Neck pain can be acute or chronic, often resulting from the complex structure of the cervical spine. Chronic pain lasting over 12 weeks often stems from prolonged poor posture ("Tech Neck"), muscular tightness, joint degeneration, or nerve root irritation. Our detailed assessment precisely identifies the source of your neck and shoulder discomfort.
