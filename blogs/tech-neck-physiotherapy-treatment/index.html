What is Tech Neck and Why Does it Cause Pain?

Tech Neck is the modern term for a repetitive stress injury where the head is held in a forward, downward position while looking at electronic devices. For every inch your head tilts forward, the weight on your spine can increase by 10-12 pounds. This massive strain on the cervical spine leads to chronic muscle fatigue, joint compression, and nerve irritation.

Common Symptoms of Tech Neck Syndrome:

  • Chronic Neck Stiffness and Pain (Cervical Pain).
  • Tension Headaches originating from the base of the skull (Cervicogenic Headache).
  • Shoulder pain and a hunched upper back (Forward Head Posture).
  • Pain radiating into the arms or hands (due to nerve compression).
  • Reduced range of motion and difficulty looking up.

The PhysioNutra Approach to Tech Neck Treatment

Our goal is to reverse the damage caused by poor posture. We combine hands-on therapies with essential strengthening and flexibility exercises. This holistic approach ensures not just pain relief, but a long-term correction of the underlying muscular and postural imbalances.

Key Treatment Components for Tech Neck Relief:

  1. Cervical Mobilization & Manual Therapy: Gentle techniques to relieve joint stiffness and improve movement in the neck and upper back.
  2. Deep Tissue Release: Targeted work on tight muscles like the Upper Trapezius, Levator Scapulae, and Scalenes to release knots and tension.
  3. Postural & Strengthening Exercises: Focused exercises to strengthen deep neck flexors and upper back extensors (the muscles responsible for holding your head back).
  4. Ergonomic Consultation: Practical advice on setting up your workspace and phone use habits to prevent recurrence.
