About the Role

We're looking for enthusiastic physiotherapy students and fresh graduates to provide high-quality home visit services to our patients across multiple cities. This role offers hands-on experience, mentorship from experienced practitioners, and the flexibility to work around your schedule.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Currently pursuing BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy) - Final year students preferred
  • Fresh BPT/MPT graduates (0-2 years experience)
  • Valid physiotherapy registration (or in process of obtaining)
  • Passionate about patient care and rehabilitation
  • Willing to travel for home visits in assigned areas
  • Own transportation (two-wheeler preferred) or willingness to use public transport

Responsibilities

  • Conduct physiotherapy sessions at patients' homes as per assigned schedules
  • Perform initial assessments and create treatment plans under supervision
  • Provide therapeutic exercises, manual therapy, and other prescribed treatments
  • Maintain detailed patient records and progress notes
  • Report to the clinic weekly for supervision and case discussions
  • Participate in training sessions and skill development workshops
  • Ensure patient safety and follow clinical protocols

What We're Looking For

  • Strong theoretical knowledge of physiotherapy principles
  • Good communication skills in English and local languages (Hindi/Punjabi)
  • Professional demeanor and empathetic approach to patient care
  • Punctuality and reliability
  • Willingness to learn and accept feedback
  • Basic smartphone skills for documentation and communication

Special Requirements

  • Clinic Visits: Candidates residing near our Zirakpur clinic or willing to visit the clinic regularly for training and supervision
  • Flexible Schedule: Ability to accommodate morning and evening appointment slots
  • Service Areas: Preference given to candidates who can cover specific city zones efficiently

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply if I'm still studying?
Yes! We welcome final-year BPT students who can manage their academic commitments along with part-time work. Many of our team members are students who work flexible hours around their class schedules.
Do I need my own vehicle?
While having your own two-wheeler is preferred for flexibility, it's not mandatory. We can arrange assignments based on public transport accessibility or provide support for transportation in certain cases.
How often do I need to visit the clinic?
Initially, you'll visit the clinic 2-3 times per week for training and supervision. Once you're established, weekly visits are required for case discussions, supervision, and collecting treatment materials. Candidates residing near Zirakpur have an advantage due to proximity.
What is the payment structure?
We offer competitive per-session payments based on the type and duration of treatment. Payment is made bi-weekly or monthly based on your preference. Performance bonuses and incentives are also provided for excellent work and patient feedback.
Will I get a certificate of experience?
Yes, absolutely! Upon successful completion of your tenure with us, you'll receive an experience certificate and reference letter that you can use for higher education applications or job opportunities.
What kind of training will I receive?
You'll receive comprehensive training including clinical protocols, patient assessment techniques, documentation procedures, safety guidelines, and communication skills. You'll also shadow experienced therapists for the first few sessions before working independently.
Can I choose my service area?
We try our best to assign you to areas close to your residence or college for convenience. During the application process, you can indicate your preferred locations, and we'll accommodate them based on patient demand in those areas.
What if I need to take a break for exams?
We understand academic commitments. You can take scheduled breaks for exams or personal reasons. Just inform us in advance so we can manage patient schedules accordingly. Many students work with us on a semester-basis arrangement.
