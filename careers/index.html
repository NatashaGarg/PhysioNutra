About the Role

We’re looking for enthusiastic physiotherapy students and fresh graduates to provide high-quality home visit services to our patients. This role offers hands-on clinical experience, direct mentorship from Dr. Tarun Garg, and the flexibility to work around your academic schedule or personal commitments.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Currently pursuing BPT — final year students preferred
  • Fresh BPT / MPT graduates (0–2 years experience)
  • Valid physiotherapy registration (or in process of obtaining)
  • Passionate about patient care and rehabilitation
  • Willing to travel for home visits in assigned areas
  • Own two-wheeler preferred, or willingness to use public transport

Responsibilities

  • Conduct physiotherapy sessions at patients’ homes as per assigned schedules
  • Perform initial assessments and create treatment plans under supervision
  • Provide therapeutic exercises, manual therapy, and other prescribed treatments
  • Maintain detailed patient records and progress notes
  • Report to the clinic weekly for supervision and case discussions
  • Participate in training sessions and skill development workshops
  • Ensure patient safety and follow clinical protocols at all times

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong theoretical knowledge of physiotherapy principles
  • Good communication skills in English and local languages (Hindi/Punjabi)
  • Professional demeanour and empathetic approach to patient care
  • Punctuality, reliability and ownership of your work
  • Willingness to learn, accept feedback, and grow
  • Basic smartphone skills for documentation and communication

Special Requirements

  • Clinic Visits: Candidates residing near Zirakpur or willing to visit the clinic regularly for training and supervision
  • Flexible Schedule: Ability to accommodate morning and evening appointment slots
  • Service Areas: Preference given to candidates who can cover specific city zones efficiently

Submit Application
