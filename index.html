Frequently Asked Questions

What treatments are available for cervical pain?
Treatments for cervical pain include manual therapy, exercises, posture correction, and pain relief modalities like heat or ultrasound.
How can physiotherapy help with chronic back pain?
Physiotherapy improves back pain by strengthening muscles, improving flexibility, correcting posture, and educating on ergonomic practices.
What causes vertigo and how is it treated?
Vertigo is often caused by inner ear problems. Treatment includes balance retraining exercises and vestibular rehabilitation therapy.
How is frozen shoulder treated?
Frozen shoulder treatment includes gentle stretching, joint mobilization, and specific exercises to restore shoulder movement.
What are effective therapies for neck pain?
Neck pain therapies include manual therapy, traction, strengthening exercises, and ergonomic advice to reduce strain.
How does sports injury rehabilitation work?
Rehab involves reducing pain and swelling, restoring movement, strengthening muscles, and gradually returning to activity under supervision.
What causes muscle spasms and how are they managed?
Muscle spasms can result from overuse, dehydration, or injury. Treatments include stretching, massage, hydration, and sometimes heat therapy.
