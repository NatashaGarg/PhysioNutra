Your Questions, Answered

What is physiotherapy and who needs it? ‚ñº

Physiotherapy is a healthcare profession that helps restore, maintain, and maximize physical strength, function, and movement. You may need physiotherapy if you're suffering from acute or chronic pain, recovering from surgery or injury, managing chronic conditions like arthritis or stroke, experiencing mobility issues, or looking to prevent future injuries. Learn more about our physiotherapy services.

Do I need a doctor's referral to book an appointment? ‚ñº

No, you don't need a referral from a doctor to visit PhysioNutra Clinic. You can book directly for an initial assessment. However, if you have recent X-rays, MRI reports, or a doctor's referral, please bring them along as they help guide the assessment and treatment plan.

How long does a typical session last, and how many will I need? ‚ñº

Each session typically lasts 45 to 60 minutes. The number of sessions needed varies by condition - acute conditions may require 5-8 sessions, while chronic conditions might need 10-15 sessions or more. After your initial assessment, your physiotherapist will discuss a recommended treatment timeline focused on the shortest path to lasting recovery.

Will physiotherapy hurt? ‚ñº

While some manual techniques or therapeutic exercises may cause temporary discomfort as you work to restore movement, your physiotherapist will always operate within your pain tolerance. Our goal is to relieve pain, not cause it. You might feel mild muscle soreness after exercise (similar to a good workout), which is normal as your body adjusts and heals.

Do you offer home physiotherapy services? ‚ñº

Yes! We offer professional home physiotherapy services across Chandigarh, Panchkula, Zirakpur, and Mohali. Our therapists bring all necessary portable equipment to provide the same high-quality care you'd receive at the clinic. Perfect for patients with limited mobility or those who prefer treatment at home.

What should I wear to my physiotherapy appointment? ‚ñº

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement. For lower body treatments, wear shorts or loose pants. For upper body/shoulder treatments, wear a tank top or t-shirt. Comfortable athletic wear is ideal. You may be asked to change into a gown for certain treatments.

Can physiotherapy help me avoid surgery? ‚ñº

In many cases, yes! Physiotherapy can help avoid surgery for conditions like mild to moderate arthritis, disc herniation, rotator cuff injuries, and knee problems. We work closely with orthopedic surgeons to provide conservative treatment first. Many of our patients have successfully avoided surgery through dedicated physiotherapy programs. Read about our orthopedic rehabilitation services.

Do you treat sports injuries? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Sports physiotherapy is one of our specialties. We treat all types of sports injuries including sprains, strains, ACL tears, meniscus injuries, rotator cuff injuries, tennis elbow, runner's knee, and more. We work with athletes from various sports to help them return to peak performance safely. Learn more about our sports injury treatment.
