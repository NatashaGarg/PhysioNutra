How We Treat Vertigo

Our Vertigo Treatment Process

The evidence-based, step-by-step approach shown in the video — and used for every patient

1

Comprehensive Vestibular Assessment

We identify the exact type and canal affected using the Dix-Hallpike maneuver, Roll test, Head Impulse Test, and balance assessments. Accurate diagnosis is essential — different vertigo types require different treatment protocols.

2

Epley Maneuver (Shown in Video)

For BPPV — the most common cause — we perform the gold-standard Epley maneuver: a precise series of head positions that repositions displaced calcium crystals (otoconia) back into the correct inner ear chamber. Safe, drug-free, and highly effective.

3

Vestibular Rehabilitation Exercises

Customised exercises to retrain the brain’s balance system: gaze stabilisation, habituation exercises, balance training, and Brandt-Daroff exercises for home practice. Critical for preventing recurrence and restoring confidence.

4

Follow-Up & Prevention

Post-treatment review to confirm crystal repositioning. Home exercise programme. Advice on sleeping positions, activity modification, and lifestyle factors that reduce recurrence risk. Open access for any return of symptoms.
