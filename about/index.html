Our Story

Built on a Simple Belief: True Healing Needs More Than Symptom Relief

PhysioNutra Clinic was founded with a clear mission — to combine the best of evidence-based physiotherapy with nutritional science to address not just symptoms, but root causes. Located in Zirakpur, we have become the trusted destination for comprehensive, holistic recovery across the Chandigarh Tricity region.

Dr. Tarun Garg established the clinic after recognising a gap in integrated care — one that treats the whole person, not just the injury. Our approach incorporates physical rehabilitation alongside nutritional support, helping thousands of patients achieve lasting results and improved quality of life.

Today, PhysioNutra Clinic serves patients from Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula, Kharar, Baltana, Dera Bassi, Dhakoli, Peer Muchalla, and Pinjore with state-of-the-art facilities and compassionate, personalised care. Read what our patients say in our testimonials or check our FAQs.
