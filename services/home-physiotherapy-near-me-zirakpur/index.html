What is Home Physiotherapy?

Expert Physiotherapy Delivered to Your Doorstep

Home physiotherapy brings the same professional treatment you receive in a clinic directly to your home. Our certified physiotherapists arrive fully equipped — with portable electrotherapy units, manual therapy tools, exercise aids, and all assessment equipment — ready to provide clinic-quality care without you needing to travel.

This service is especially valuable for patients who have recently undergone surgery, elderly individuals with mobility challenges, stroke survivors, and anyone managing a condition that makes commuting difficult or painful.

Key Advantages

Why patients prefer home physiotherapy

  • No travel stress — treatment in your familiar, comfortable environment
  • Family members can observe and learn assistance techniques
  • One-on-one focused attention without clinic distractions
  • Reduced infection risk, especially post-surgery
  • Exercises designed around your actual home environment
  • Higher treatment adherence and faster recovery outcomes

  Research consistently shows patients treated at home have better adherence and equivalent or superior outcomes compared to clinic-only care — especially for elderly and post-surgical populations.

How It Works

Your Home Physiotherapy Journey

Simple, convenient, and professional — from your first call to full recovery

1

Call or WhatsApp to Book (Day 0)

Contact us at +91 94177 91833 or WhatsApp. Share your condition, location, and preferred time. We confirm your appointment — often same-day for urgent cases. No lengthy intake forms or waiting lists.

2

Physiotherapist Arrives at Your Home

Our physiotherapist arrives on time with all necessary equipment. You need only a comfortable space (bed or floor mat), loose clothing, and any medical reports or prescriptions. We set up quickly and professionally.

3

Comprehensive First Assessment

A thorough evaluation covering your medical history, current condition, range of motion, strength, pain levels, and functional limitations. We also review your home layout to identify safety risks and plan environment-appropriate exercises.

4

Personalised Treatment Plan Created

Based on your assessment, we design a customised programme with specific goals and measurable milestones. We explain everything clearly to you and your family so everyone understands the recovery roadmap.

5

Regular Treatment Sessions (45–60 min each)

Each session includes manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, electrotherapy where indicated, and education. Family members are coached in proper assistance techniques. Your programme is adjusted based on your progress at every visit.

6

Progress Review & Long-Term Support

Regular objective assessments track your recovery. You receive updated home exercise programmes as you improve. Between sessions, WhatsApp access to your therapist ensures continuity and confidence throughout your recovery.
