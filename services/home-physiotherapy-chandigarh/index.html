Can family members be present during home physiotherapy sessions?

+
Absolutely! We strongly encourage family involvement during home physiotherapy sessions. Having family members present helps them understand the patient's condition better, learn proper techniques to assist with exercises between sessions, provide emotional support, and ensure exercises are performed correctly at home. This family participation is especially valuable for elderly patients, stroke survivors, post-surgical care, and pediatric cases, and often leads to significantly better treatment outcomes and faster recovery.

+
Yes! Home physiotherapy can be equally or even more effective than clinic treatment. You receive the same certified physiotherapists, professional equipment, and evidence-based treatment protocols.
