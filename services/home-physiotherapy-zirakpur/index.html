What equipment do you bring for home sessions?

+
Our physiotherapists come fully equipped with professional tools needed for effective home treatment. Equipment includes: exercise bands and resistance tubes, therapy balls and weights, TENS machine for pain relief, ultrasound therapy device, hot/cold packs, foam rollers, balance training equipment, blood pressure monitor, goniometer for range of motion measurement, and treatment mat. All equipment is sterilized and maintained to highest hygiene standards. We bring everything needed for comprehensive treatment - you don't need to purchase or arrange anything. For some advanced modalities requiring larger equipment, we may recommend occasional clinic visits.

How long is each home physiotherapy session?

+
Each home physiotherapy session typically lasts 45-60 minutes. This includes initial assessment (first session only), treatment with manual therapy and exercises, use of pain relief modalities as needed, patient education, and home exercise program instruction. First session may take slightly longer (60-75 minutes) as it includes comprehensive evaluation, medical history review, and treatment plan development. Subsequent sessions focus on treatment and progress monitoring. Session duration may be adjusted based on patient's condition, tolerance, and specific treatment needs. We ensure adequate time for quality treatment without rushing.

How many sessions will I need?

+
Number of sessions depends on your condition, severity, and treatment goals. Typical ranges: Acute pain/minor injuries: 5-8 sessions over 2-3 weeks. Post-surgery rehabilitation: 12-20 sessions over 2-3 months. Chronic conditions (arthritis, back pain): 10-15 sessions initially, then maintenance sessions as needed. Stroke recovery: 20-40 sessions over 3-6 months. Elderly mobility issues: Ongoing regular sessions (2-3x weekly). After initial assessment, we'll provide realistic timeline and session recommendations. Progress is monitored regularly and plan adjusted as needed. Many patients see significant improvement within first 6-8 sessions.

Can family members observe the sessions?

+
Absolutely! We encourage family involvement. Having family members observe sessions is highly beneficial as they can: learn proper exercise techniques to help patient between sessions, understand the treatment plan and recovery timeline, assist with positioning and transfers safely, provide emotional support and motivation, and ask questions about care and precautions. This is especially important for elderly patients, stroke recovery, and post-surgery cases where family support significantly improves outcomes. We'll teach caregivers specific techniques and exercises to continue between our visits. Family education is an integral part of our home physiotherapy service.

Do you provide physiotherapy for COVID recovery?

+
Yes, we provide specialized post-COVID rehabilitation at home. Many COVID-19 survivors experience lingering symptoms including breathing difficulty, fatigue, weakness, joint pain, and reduced exercise capacity. Our post-COVID physiotherapy includes: breathing exercises to improve lung function, graduated exercise program to rebuild stamina, strength training to combat weakness, balance and coordination exercises, energy conservation techniques, and monitoring of oxygen saturation during activities. We follow strict safety protocols including PPE use, sanitization, and symptom screening. Treatment usually starts 2-3 weeks after recovery once cleared by doctor. Sessions help accelerate recovery and return to normal activities safely. Contact us for post-COVID rehabilitation assessment.
