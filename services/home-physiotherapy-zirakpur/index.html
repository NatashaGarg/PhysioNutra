What is Home Physiotherapy?

Professional Physiotherapy Delivered to Your Doorstep in Zirakpur

Home physiotherapy brings the same quality of clinical treatment directly to your home. Our certified physiotherapists visit with professional portable equipment, provide personalised hands-on treatment, and leave you with a structured exercise programme to follow between sessions.

It is ideal for elderly patients, anyone recovering from surgery or stroke, individuals with mobility restrictions, or simply those who prefer the comfort and convenience of home-based care. Dr. Tarun Garg’s team covers all localities across Zirakpur and greater Chandigarh Tricity.

How It Works

Our Home Physiotherapy Process

A simple, clear pathway from your first call to complete recovery

1

Book Your Home Visit (Call or WhatsApp)

Call +91 94177 91833 or WhatsApp us. Share your location in Zirakpur, your condition, and your preferred time slot. We confirm your appointment — often same day or next day.

2

Initial Assessment at Your Home

Our certified physiotherapist visits your home with professional equipment. A thorough assessment is conducted — medical history review, functional testing, pain evaluation, and goal-setting. A personalised treatment plan is created on Day 1.

3

Regular Treatment Sessions

Sessions are 45–60 minutes each. Treatment includes manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, pain relief modalities (TENS, ultrasound), functional training, and patient education. Family members can observe and learn.

4

Home Exercise Programme

Between visits, you follow a written home exercise programme tailored to your condition and environment. Our therapists check adherence and adjust exercises at every session to maintain progress.

5

Progress Review & Milestone Tracking

We conduct formal progress reviews every 4–6 sessions. Objective measurements (strength, ROM, balance, functional tests) track your improvement. The programme is updated based on results — not assumptions.

6

Recovery & Long-Term Maintenance

Once your primary goals are achieved, we provide a long-term maintenance plan and open access for any queries or concerns. For elderly or chronic patients, we offer ongoing regular sessions to sustain gains.

Home physiotherapy should ideally begin within 24–48 hours of hospital discharge after orthopaedic surgeries. Early rehabilitation controls swelling, prevents stiffness, restores range of motion, and accelerates overall recovery. We coordinate directly with your surgeon to initiate safe, timely treatment at home.
