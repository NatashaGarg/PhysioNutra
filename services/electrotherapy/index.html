What is Electrotherapy?

Evidence-Based Electrotherapy for Pain Relief & Faster Healing

Electrotherapy uses controlled electrical energy for therapeutic purposes — reducing pain, stimulating muscles, improving circulation, and accelerating tissue healing. At PhysioNutra Clinic, Dr. Tarun Aggarwal combines five advanced modalities — TENS, IFT, therapeutic ultrasound, Class IV LASER, and EMS — into personalised treatment programmes rooted in current clinical evidence.

Unlike pain medications that mask symptoms, electrotherapy works with your body's own mechanisms: stimulating endorphin release, enhancing cellular repair, and restoring normal neuromuscular function. It is safe, non-invasive, and highly effective for both acute injuries and chronic pain conditions.

Is Electrotherapy Right for You?

Signs you may benefit from electrotherapy

  • Persistent back, neck, or joint pain not responding to rest
  • Muscle weakness or wasting after injury or surgery
  • Nerve pain, tingling, or numbness (sciatica, neuropathy)
  • Inflammation and swelling around joints or tendons
  • Slow-healing soft tissue injuries (tendons, ligaments, muscles)
  • Reliance on pain medications with unwanted side effects

 Electrotherapy is contraindicated with pacemakers, over active cancer sites, during pregnancy, and in epilepsy. Our physiotherapist conducts a full safety screening before every first session.

Treatment Modalities

Our Electrotherapy Services

Five evidence-based modalities, each selected and calibrated by Dr. Tarun Aggarwal for your specific condition

TENS Therapy

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation

Low-voltage electrical currents delivered via skin electrodes that block pain signals to the brain and trigger natural endorphin release for lasting relief.

  • Chronic and acute pain management
  • Non-addictive alternative to pain medication
  • Effective for back pain, arthritis, nerve pain
  • Session duration: 20–30 minutes

IFT Therapy

Interferential Therapy

Medium-frequency currents from two channels that intersect deep within tissues, providing stronger, deeper pain relief than TENS with less skin discomfort.

  • Deep tissue pain and inflammation
  • Sports injuries and joint pain
  • Improves blood circulation significantly
  • Session duration: 15–20 minutes

Ultrasound Therapy

Therapeutic Ultrasound

High-frequency sound waves generate deep thermal and mechanical effects that accelerate tissue repair, reduce scar formation, and relieve muscle spasm.

  • Tendonitis and ligament injuries
  • Scar tissue and adhesion breakdown
  • Enhanced tissue repair at cellular level
  • Session duration: 5–10 minutes

LASER Therapy

Class IV Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)

Specific wavelengths of light penetrate tissues to stimulate cellular metabolism, reduce inflammation, and accelerate healing without heat or discomfort.

  • Wound healing and nerve regeneration
  • Significant anti-inflammatory effect
  • Painless, non-thermal treatment
  • Session duration: 5–15 minutes

EMS Therapy

Electrical Muscle Stimulation

Electrical impulses cause targeted muscle contractions to prevent atrophy, rebuild strength, and restore normal neuromuscular control after injury or surgery.

  • Post-surgical muscle rehabilitation
  • Prevents and reverses muscle wasting
  • Improves muscle tone and function
  • Session duration: 15–25 minutes

Combination Therapy

Multimodal Electrotherapy Programme

Strategic combination of two or more modalities within a single session, alongside manual therapy and exercise, for superior outcomes in complex conditions.

  • Tailored to your specific diagnosis
  • Integrates with manual therapy and exercise
  • Optimal for chronic or complex pain
  • Personalised session plan created for you

Your Journey

What to Expect at Your Electrotherapy Sessions

A safe, structured approach from first contact to full recovery

1

Comprehensive Assessment (Session 1)

Thorough evaluation of your condition, pain severity, medical history, contraindications, and treatment goals. We explain the selected modality, what sensations to expect, and create a personalised session schedule with clear outcome milestones.

2

Safety Screening

We screen for all contraindications before treatment — pacemakers, implanted devices, pregnancy, active cancer, epilepsy, and skin conditions. Your safety is the absolute priority. If electrotherapy is not appropriate, we recommend the best alternative treatment.

3

Preparation & Set-Up

Electrodes, applicators, or probes are precisely positioned on or near the treatment area. Equipment parameters (frequency, intensity, waveform) are calibrated for your condition. You are comfortably positioned throughout.

4

Active Treatment Session

The electrotherapy device is activated. Most patients experience gentle tingling, pulsing, warmth, or mild muscle contractions — all within your comfort range. We monitor throughout and adjust parameters as needed. Sessions last 5–30 minutes depending on the modality.

5

Progress Monitoring & Plan Adjustment

Regular re-assessments track your progress. Treatment parameters and session frequency are adjusted as your condition improves. Acute conditions typically complete in 3–5 sessions; chronic conditions in 8–12. We combine with manual therapy and exercises for comprehensive care.
