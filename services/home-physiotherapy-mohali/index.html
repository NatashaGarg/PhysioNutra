Our Process

How Home Physiotherapy Works in Mohali

Simple, seamless, and completely focused on your recovery — from first call to full function

1

Book Your Appointment

Call +91 94177 91833 or WhatsApp us with your phase or sector in Mohali and a preferred time. We confirm your slot within the hour — same-day visits available across most of Mohali.

2

Initial Home Assessment

Your certified physiotherapist arrives at your home in Mohali with all necessary equipment. A thorough assessment covers your medical history, current pain levels, movement limitations, relevant investigations, and recovery goals. A personalised treatment plan is built on the spot.

3

Treatment Sessions Begin

Scheduled regular sessions at your home. Each visit includes hands-on therapy, electrotherapy (TENS/ultrasound where indicated), guided exercises, and family carer education. All equipment is carried by the therapist — you need nothing extra.

4

Progress Monitoring & Plan Adjustment

Your progress is measured at every session against objective milestones — strength, range of motion, pain scores, and functional ability. Your programme is updated at each visit based on your response, not on a fixed time schedule.

5

Discharge & Long-Term Maintenance

Once your goals are achieved, you receive a personalised home exercise programme for continued health and prevention. We remain available for follow-up, questions, or any new concerns — long-term wellbeing is our goal, not just a short treatment episode.
