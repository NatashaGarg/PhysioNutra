How Home Physiotherapy Packages Work

PhysioNutra Clinic offers structured home visit physiotherapy packages so patients recovering from surgery, injury, or age-related mobility loss can get consistent, professional care without travelling to a clinic.

Packages are available with a junior physiotherapist or a senior physiotherapist, in 10-day, 15-day, or 30-day durations. Longer packages work out to a lower effective cost per day. All packages require full advance payment as a one-time payment before the visits begin.

Quick Answer

PhysioNutra Clinic's home physiotherapy packages start at ₹700/day with a junior physiotherapist (10-day: ₹6,300 · 15-day: ₹8,925 · 30-day: ₹16,800) and ₹1,000/day with a senior physiotherapist (10-day: ₹9,000 · 15-day: ₹12,750 · 30-day: ₹24,000). Advance one-time payment required. Serving Zirakpur, Chandigarh, Mohali & Panchkula. Call +91 94177 91833.

Who Home Visits Suit

Home physiotherapy packages are ideal if you are:

  • Recovering post-surgery and unable to travel comfortably
  • An elderly patient needing consistent, supervised care at home
  • Managing a chronic condition that benefits from regular sessions
  • Looking for a fixed daily rate over a set number of days
  • A caregiver arranging care for a family member across Tricity

 Not sure which tier or duration fits your condition? Call +91 94177 91833 — Dr. Tarun Garg's team will recommend the right package after understanding your case.
