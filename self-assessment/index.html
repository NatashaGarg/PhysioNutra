Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our assessment tools

How accurate are these assessments?
Our assessments are based on evidence-based clinical diagnostic criteria and developed by experienced physiotherapists. While they provide highly accurate preliminary diagnosis (85-90% correlation with clinical diagnosis), they are meant to guide you and should be confirmed by a physical examination by a qualified professional.
Do I need to register or pay to use these tools?
No! All our assessment tools are completely free and require no registration. Simply choose your assessment, answer the questions, and get instant results. No hidden charges, ever.
How long does each assessment take?
Most assessments take 2-3 minutes to complete. They contain 7-10 questions designed to be quick yet comprehensive enough to provide accurate results.
Can I take the assessment multiple times?
Yes, absolutely! You can retake any assessment as many times as you want. This is helpful to track your progress or if your symptoms change over time.
What happens after I complete an assessment?
You'll receive instant results showing: your severity level, possible conditions, personalized treatment recommendations, expected recovery timeline, and options to book an appointment with our physiotherapists. You can also save or share your results.
Are my responses confidential?
Yes, your privacy is important to us. Your responses are confidential and used only to generate your personalized results. We never share your information without explicit consent.
Can these assessments replace a doctor's visit?
No. These assessments are educational tools designed to help you understand your symptoms better. They do NOT replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult with a qualified physiotherapist or doctor for proper examination, diagnosis, and treatment.
What if my results show serious symptoms?
If the assessment detects red flag symptoms (signs of serious conditions requiring immediate attention), you'll receive clear instructions to seek urgent medical care. We prioritize your safety and will always recommend emergency care when necessary.
Do you offer treatment after assessment?
Yes! PhysioNutra Clinic offers comprehensive physiotherapy treatment for all conditions covered in our assessments. After completing your assessment, you can easily book an appointment by calling +91 94177 91833 or via WhatsApp. We serve Zirakpur, Chandigarh, Mohali, Panchkula and surrounding areas.
